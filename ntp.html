Helpful Tips - NTP

  • Make sure to distinguish between a Scope of Work OR Statement of Work.
  • Projects over $10,000 require 3 bids. Over $150,000 also require a Gov. IGE.
  • All projects require an SOW, Request for Proposal and a Proposal.
  • If you have a C#, enter the # into the 'Project Information' field in the General Information tab.
  • If the POP exceeds the lease term: Double check the Lease Matrix. If the expiration dates match, then adjust the POP of the MOD to end the day prior to lease expiration. Let the CO know of this change in the comments section when routing.
